Tammy Hoskins-Robinson is a 32 year old government employee from Philadelphia, PA. Her desire to be a part of the tech world started at Indiana University of PA, where she majored
in engineering. Although trying to break into a field of study that wasn't popular amoungst women, she remained determined to complete the course. She finished and graduated, being the first in her family to do so.

After graduation, Tammy returned to Philadelphia and worked as a network engineer for a telecommunications company in the King of Prussia area of the state. Her experience there taught her that she had to work hard to become an asset so that it would be difficult for employers to release her when they had to downsize. However, she was unable to avoid the decision her employer made to outsource her position overseas. This did not deter her in any way as she sought to find her way to her next job.

After being hired to replace a retired employee at a State Senators' office, Tammy knew she had to make this a steppingstone towards what she is destined to do in life. Reading about New York Code & Design Academy after searching for coding schools in Philadelphia, seemed almost like fate. The decision to then enroll in a Web Development course was just the beginning of her transition to becoming a full stack developer. She is committed to making that into a reality so stay tuned.
